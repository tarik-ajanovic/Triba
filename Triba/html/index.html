TRIBA

The game consists of an mxn grid of points, where m and n are even natural numbers. Two players take turns. In each turn, a player chooses a triangle by selecting three points from the grid that are not on the same line. The triangles may not intersect, and no two triangles may have a common vertex. The triangles may be nested within each other. The game is over when one player has no moves left. The player with no moves left loses.
